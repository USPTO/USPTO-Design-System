Purpose

Custom scrollbar

Custom scrollbar variants

Custom scrollbar include their own variant classes for quickly changing the look and feel of a scrollbar. Different custom scrollbar require the use of .scrollbar-custom-* {primary, success, warning, info, secondary, danger, light, dark }.
